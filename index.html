Auto Stepless Transmission

Cycling up or down a hill? You set your preferred cadence, and the transmission is controlled automatically so that you can always pedal at the same pace. Our continuously variable transmission system can change seamlessly through a continuous range of effective gear ratios, making riding effortless as never before.

Full Control At Your Fingertips

An easy-to-use interface gives you full control of bicycle parameters. Select from three distinct operating modes. Use the Workbench to sculpt the primary characteristics of your bike.
